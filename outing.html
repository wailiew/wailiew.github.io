My Hiking Trip

Outing

While I'm not studying/working or sitting in front of my computer, I like to take a long walk and go on hikes with my dog. I live near a huge park and it gives me a lot of hiking trails to walk my dog. Also, it has many open fields that allow my dog to run around in. Playing fetch with my dog is one of my favorite things to do, it brings me a kind of joy that is incomparable.

Like many CS students, we are all glued to the computer because that's where we get our work done and sometimes we forget to get up and stretch. Taking long walks also helps me take a step back and think about the problem I have in hand. Sometimes when I'm having difficulties with a problem I like to take a step back to think about it. I may not find the solution every time I take a step back but coming back to the problem with a different mindset might help.
