A brief background

I am a Queens College graduate with a degree in Computer Science.

My passion for coding started when I attended my first CS class "Introduction to Algorithmic Problem-Solving" in Queens College. I always thought I was computer savvy but this was a challenge that I never experienced. I took a bit long but after a while I got the hang of it and every problem that I'm solving became a puzzle to me. I realized that there are many ways to solve a puzzle and instead of seeing it as homework/projects that I need to complete, it became a fun challenge that I want to solve.

I'm truly passionate about taking on challenges that are out of my comfort zone. As a self-starter and avid learner, I regularly work on challenge from leetcode and hackerrank and take on classes from Udemy.
